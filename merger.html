Ocelot Security and Revolver Industries Complete Strategic Merger

Austin, TX — Ocelot Security, a leader in enterprise cybersecurity consulting, and Revolver Industries, a fast-growing managed infrastructure provider, today announced the completion of their strategic merger. The combined organization will operate under a unified technology platform, bringing together Ocelot’s deep security expertise with Revolver’s cloud infrastructure capabilities.

“This merger positions us to deliver end-to-end security and infrastructure solutions that neither company could offer alone.” — Sarah Chen, CEO

The combined company will retain the Ocelot Security brand and operate from its Austin headquarters. All Revolver Industries employees have been onboarded to the unified Entra ID tenant, and IT migration is underway. A dedicated merger migration team has been established to ensure continuity of operations during the transition period.

“Joining forces with Ocelot gives our team access to world-class security talent. Our clients will benefit immediately.” — Elena Santos, Director of Operations, Revolver Industries
